Personalize your study-abroad journey

We are committed to assisting students in selecting the best match universities, colleges and high schools based on your background in Australia and New Zealand. Go abroad Study is to provide professional advice and updated school information that cater to your academic background, financial condition, and other requirements. Our highly committed educational agent aims to guide you to find an educational journey that only belongs to you.

Enrich your studying oversea life

Studying abroad is not an easy thing. It is about time you have the opportunity to grow independently and manage yourself in each aspect of life. We not only focus on helping you deal with the study application but also provide supportive student services to every new student. Additionally, our responsible educational agents are here to give students must-know information while studying abroad.

A wide range of additional student services

We dedicate to take care of every aspect of students’ learning needs. We provide English assessment proofread services, visa applications, insurance, homestay, and accommodation and pick-up airport services. You don’ have to worry when you arrive in the new country because we are here to look after you.
